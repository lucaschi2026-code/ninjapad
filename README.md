# Ninjapad
Ninjapad is a macropad with 9 keys, 1 rotary encoder, and an 0.91 inch OLED display. It uses a custom PCB, 3D printed case, and QMK firmware.

## BOM
- 1 Seeed XIAO RP2040
- 9 through-hole 1N4148 Diodes
- 9 MX-Style switches
- 1 EC11 Rotary encoders
- 1 0.91 inch OLED display
- 9 white blank DSA keycaps
- 4 M3x16mm screws
- 4 M3x5mx4mm heatset inserts
- 4 M3x5mm screws
- 1 case (top and bottom shell)
